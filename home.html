My "Description of Self"

Some might posit that the logical progression from traditional art (painting, specifically) would be to design (something involving the aesthetic arrangement and coordination of objects in two dimensions). Yeah, sure--I do that, too. But my paradigm shift is to programming. The development of web applications. I'm an aspiring full-stack developer. Explanation: "stack" may be thought of as layers; a full-stack web developer has expertise in all layers of a website’s development, including, but not limited to: the server, client and hosting, a form of data structuring or modeling, user interface and experience... Also, there's the purpose of the proposed application, begging for consideration. So many petulant little things that need tending to. I try to be good to them all, though. It's mostly a mutually copacetic affair.

I mess around in multiple creative disciplines, including painting and drawing, mixed media and installation, writing and music. I participate in logical and analytical thinking a lot, too. I tell myself this lifestyle will eventually synchronize my two brain hemispheres.
My wonderful musician boyfriend and I live with our two lovely cats, Damo Dali Dhalgren and Erykah aDuck Dhalgren (twins), in a part of America where stone walls and wealthy people are overabundant.
